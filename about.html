Who we are

Andrew is a Civil Engineering Technologist with a C.E.T. designation from O.A.C.E.T.T., and has been inspecting municipal sewer, watermains and road/bridges for over 20 years. He has also been inspecting residential properties as a Home Inspector since 1998. He attending the Part 8 Ontario Building Code - On-Site Sewage Systems Installer/Inspector Licensing Course and Pumps and Controls course at the University of Guelph and wrote and passed the exam for BCIN in the Fall of 2013.
He has been repairing/installing siding and roofing and repairing basement cracks and water leakage for more over 5 years.

What we do

We are an Experienced contractor with Engineering Background providing the highest quality work.
We offer roofing repairs and Home maintenance to fit all budgets, siding repairs/installation, skylights, roof leaks, rotten porch columns replaced, basement cracks. We provide building envelope services to keep water out of your house.
We install Bathroom and Kitchen exhaust fans complete with all inside and exterior work and seperate switch.
We offer services to seal and waterproof foundation walls, basement cracks, active leaks, form tie holes bowing walls structural cracks. We can repair from the inside by epoxy injection or polyurethane. All types and sizes of cracks can be repaired. Active leaks and around pipes. We can install perimeter drainage systems and sump pumps.
Excavation of exterior foundation walls and waterproofing also available.
